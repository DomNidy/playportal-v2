---
title: Upgrade your plan
description: Learn how to upgrade your subscription
---

You can upgrade your subscription at any time. To upgrade your subscription, follow these steps:

1. Login to your account
2. Click on your profile icon in the top right corner, then click on "Account", alternatively <a href="/dashboard/account">click here</a>.
3. Click on the `Manage Billing/Subscription` button
4. Click on the `Update Plan` button, then select the plan you would like to upgrade to.
