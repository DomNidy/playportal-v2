---
title: Change payment method
description: Learn how to change your payment method
---

You can change your payment method at any time. To change your payment method, follow these steps:

1. Login to your account
2. Click on your profile icon in the top right corner, then click on "Account", alternatively <a href="/dashboard/account">click here</a>.
3. Click on the `Manage Billing/Subscription` button

This will take you to the billing page where you can update your payment information.
