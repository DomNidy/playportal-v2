---
title: Cancel your subscription
description: Learn how to cancel your subscription
---

You can cancel your subscription at any time, and your plan will remain active until the end of the billing cycle.
To cancel your subscription, follow these steps:

1. Login to your account
2. Click on your profile icon in the top right corner, then click on "Account", alternatively <a href="/dashboard/account">click here</a>.
3. Click on the `Manage Billing/Subscription` button

This will take you to the billing page where you can cancel your subscription, change your plan, or update your payment information.
