In this guide, you will learn how to download your created videos from Playportal.

### Things you will need
1. First you will need to have created a video. If you have not created a video yet, please follow the guide on [how to create a video](/support/creating/create-a-video).

### Steps to download a video

From the Playportal [dashboard](/dashboard), you will see a list of your created videos, click on the `...` icon on the top right. 
This will open up a menu that will allow you to:

- Download the video
- View all files associated with the video (audio, image, video)
- Delete the video from Playportal
