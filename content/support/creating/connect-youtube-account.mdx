In this guide, you will learn how to connect a YouTube account with Playportal.

### Things you will need
- Google Account with a YouTube channel associated with it.

### Steps to link a YouTube Account.
1. Login to your account, and go to the dashboard
2. Click on your profile icon in the top right corner, then click on "Account", alternatively <a href="/dashboard/account">click here</a>.
3. Scroll down the account panel until you see the "Connect YouTube Account" button, then click on it.
4. Proceed with the Google prompt, you will then be redirected back to Playportal upon completion.

### An error occured while trying to link my account
This can happen when you try to link an account which has not created a YouTube channel yet.
